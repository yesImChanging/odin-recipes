Kathiyawari Cholay

Description

Kathiawari Cholay is a very delicious snack cum lunch food very tasty and tangy. origin from Memon Kathiawar, the Kathiawari community is living in Karachi. They migrated from Kathiawar - India to Karachi during the creation of Pakistan. Pushcart vendors are selling these Kathiawari Cholay in many parts of Karachi city. This is also called the street food of Karachi, mainly it is a wholesome, easy and delicious meal

Ingredients

  • White Chickpeas - 250 Grams
  • Salt - To Taste
  • Red Chili Powder - 1 Teaspoon
  • Red Chili Flakes - 1 Teaspoon
  • Cumin Powder - 1 Teaspoon
  • Chaat Masala - 1 Teaspoon
  • Chili Garlic Sauce - 1 Teaspoon
  • Tamarind Pulp - 1-2 Tablespoons
  • Cornflour Paste - 2 Tablespoons
  • Water - As Required
  • Boiled Potato - Cubed
  • Tomato - Chopped
  • Onion - Chopped - 2 Tablespoons
  • Curry Leaves - 10-12
  • Green Chilies - Chopped - 2
  • Fresh Coriander - 1/2 Cup

Steps

  1. Soak white chickpeas for 6 to 8 hours or leave overnight.
  2. In a pan, put the chickpeas and water, and boil for 45 minutes on medium flame.
  3. When the chickpeas are soft, add salt, red chili powder, red chili flakes, cumin powder, chaat masala, ketchup and mix well.
  4. Now add in tamarind pulp and cornflour paste. Mix well to cook until the gravy thickens.
  5. Pour enough water to the pan to make gravy of desired consistency.
  6. Toss in chopped boiled potato, tomatoes, onions, curry leaves and give it a quick stir.
  7. Garnish with chopped green chilies, fresh coriander, and serve hot.
  8. Enjoy!
